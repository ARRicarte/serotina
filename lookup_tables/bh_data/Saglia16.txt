Column 8: flag M, M = 0 indicates the special case of the Milky Way (see text), M = 1 indicates that the M/L was computed from the distribution function or Schwarzschild modeling, M = 2 from Jeans modeling of stellar kinematics profiles, M = 3 from Jeans modeling of central stellar velocity dispersions, M = 4 from gas dynamics.
